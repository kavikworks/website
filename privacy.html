Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Robot Arms LLC, doing business as Kavik Works ("we," "us," or "our"), respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website (kavikworks.com) and services.

Information you provide: When you contact us, request services, or sign up for our offerings, we may collect your name, email address, company name, website URL, and any other information you choose to share.

Automatically collected information: When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information including your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited. This data is collected through standard web server logs and analytics.

Payment information: We do not directly collect or store payment card information. All payments are processed through Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Paddle's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services

  • Process payments and fulfill orders

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Third parties who help us operate our business (payment processors, hosting providers, email services), bound by confidentiality obligations.

Our AI agents may process client data as part of delivering services. Client data processed by AI agents is:

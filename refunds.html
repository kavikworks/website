Refund Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

We want you to be satisfied with our services. This policy outlines when and how refunds are available for services purchased through Kavik Works LLC.

1. Service-Based Refunds

Service TypeRefund WindowConditions
Website Audits 48 hours after delivery Full refund if the audit report was not delivered or contained materially incorrect results due to our error.
AI Agent Development 48 hours after SOW signing Full refund if cancelled within 48 hours of signing. After 48 hours, refunds are prorated based on work completed.
Monthly Retainers Pro-rated by days unused Cancel anytime. You'll be billed only for the days in the current billing period that have elapsed.
AI-Generated Content 7 days after delivery Refund available if the delivered content materially fails to meet the specifications in the project brief.

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally non-refundable:

  • Services that have been fully delivered and accepted by the client
  • Third-party costs incurred on your behalf (API usage, hosting fees, software licenses)
  • Rush delivery fees once work has commenced
  • Custom development work beyond the 48-hour cancellation window

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email josh@kavikworks.com with your order details and reason for the request. We'll review your request within 2 business days and respond with a determination.

4. Processing

Approved refunds are processed through Paddle, our payment processor, and typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days, depending on your financial institution.

5. Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We're committed to resolving issues fairly and quickly. Chargebacks initiated without prior contact may result in termination of services.

6. Questions

For questions about this refund policy, contact josh@kavikworks.com.

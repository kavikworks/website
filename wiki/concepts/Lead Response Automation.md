# Lead Response Automation

The core productized offering of [[Kavik Works]]. A single well-scoped automation that monitors a business inbox, classifies incoming leads with AI, and responds within minutes — replacing hours of manual triage per week.

## What It Does

1. Monitors the client's Gmail or Outlook inbox continuously
2. Classifies each inbound email: lead inquiry, existing customer, spam, other
3. Extracts contact details and key request information
4. Sends a personalized acknowledgement to the sender within minutes
5. Routes the lead to the right person or queue
6. Logs everything to a CRM or spreadsheet

## Why This Product

- **Proven ROI:** The lead response time problem is well-studied with clear benchmarks (see below)
- **Simple to scope:** One inbox, one workflow — no per-client surprises
- **Runs autonomously:** No human in the loop for routine responses
- **Repeatable:** Same implementation pattern across industries

## Fit Criteria

**Strong fit signals:**
- 10+ inbound email inquiries per week
- Losing deals to slow response (self-reported or implied)
- Gmail or Outlook as the primary inbox
- Professional services, agencies, contractors, real estate, healthcare admin, trades

**Poor fit signals:**
- Fewer than 10 leads/week (retainer math doesn't work)
- Leads arrive by phone only
- Industry with strict outbound communication compliance
- Response time already under 1 hour consistently
- Expects personal relationship management

## Monthly Automated Report

Every client receives one email per month (generated by Claude, no manual work) showing:
- Total leads handled that month
- Average response time vs. 42-hr industry average
- Estimated hours saved
- $ value of time saved (at $35/hr)
- System health status

This replaces all scheduled quarterly reviews. 20 clients = 20 automated emails/month, not 80 scheduled calls/year.

## Industry Benchmarks

These are cited in every feasibility report:

- **InsideSales Research:** Average business response time is 42 hours

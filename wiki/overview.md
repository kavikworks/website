# Kavik Works — Business Overview

## What We Do

[[Kavik Works]] is a one-person automation consultancy. We build AI-powered inbox automations for small and mid-sized businesses — primarily the [[Lead Response Automation]] product, which turns a manual lead triage workflow into a self-running system.

## Where We Are (April 2026)

- **Product:** Single core product launched and pipeline fully operational
- **Pipeline:** intake form → AI feasibility report → email to Josh, end-to-end in ~35 seconds, no manual work
- **Deployments:** Apps Script Version 8 live; 2 test scenarios verified (Acme Corp strong fit, Bob's Carpentry poor fit)
- **Clients:** Pre-launch — pipeline is ready to accept real inquiries
- **Revenue:** $0 MRR (pre-revenue)

## Key Strategic Decisions (April 2026)

1. **Single product, not a roadmap.** Replaced the multi-phase Pilot/Standard/Growth model with one well-scoped core product. Eliminates per-client scoping uncertainty.

2. **High-ticket add-ons only.** Add-ons start at $2,500 setup + $400/mo. Not cheap experiments. Each must have an unambiguous ROI case before Josh scopes it.

3. **Autonomous by design.** No quarterly reviews, no scheduled calls. Monthly automated reports substitute for all scheduled touchpoints. Designed to serve 20+ clients without 80 scheduled calls/year.

4. **Fit-first intake.** The AI feasibility report exists to protect Josh's time as much as the client's. Poor fit = do not pursue, stated plainly in the report.

## Metrics to Watch

- Number of intake form submissions
- Fit score distribution (how many strong vs. poor fit)
- Pipeline conversion rate (inquiries → signed clients)
- MRR (target: 10 clients × $497 = $4,970/mo)
- Avg response time for client automations (should stay < 5 min)
